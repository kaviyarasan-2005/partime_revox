Finding Us

We are located in the historic red brick building next to the Artisan Coffee Roasters. Look for the large terracotta sign.

Parking

Free 2-hour street parking is available along Artisan Way. For longer sessions, there is a paid lot directly behind our building on 4th Ave.

Accessibility

Our studio is on the ground floor with zero steps from the sidewalk. We have two ADA-compliant adjustable height pottery wheels.
