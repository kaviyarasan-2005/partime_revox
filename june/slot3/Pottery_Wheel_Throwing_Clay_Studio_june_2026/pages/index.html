The Studio Experience

Our sunlit, 3,000 sq ft studio is designed to inspire. Whether you're a seasoned ceramicist or touching clay for the first time, you'll find a supportive environment equipped with everything you need.

  • 20 Professional Shimpo Wheels
  • 3 Large capacity electric kilns
  • Dedicated glazing room with 40+ custom house glazes
  • Open studio access for members 7 days a week
