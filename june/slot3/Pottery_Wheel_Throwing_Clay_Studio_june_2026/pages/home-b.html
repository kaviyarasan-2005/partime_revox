Professional Firing Services

Bisque Firing

Low-fire cone 04. Transforms your greenware into porous, durable ceramic ready for glazing. Turnaround is typically 3-5 days.

Glaze Firing

High-fire cone 6. Melts the glaze into a glass-like coating, sealing the piece and bringing your colors to life. 5-7 day turnaround.

Specialty Raku

A dramatic, rapid firing process creating unique metallic and crackle effects. Hosted once a month in our outdoor courtyard.
