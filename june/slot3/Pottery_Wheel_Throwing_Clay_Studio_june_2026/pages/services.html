Class Details & Curriculum

Deep dive into what you'll learn in our most popular programs.

This comprehensive 6-week course is designed for absolute beginners. We cover the entire process from raw clay to finished, glazed pieces. Expect to make cylinders, bowls, and simple cups.

Curriculum:
  • Week 1: Wedging and Centering
  • Week 2: Pulling Cylinders
  • Week 3: Shaping Bowls
  • Week 4: Trimming and Footing
  • Week 5: Handles and Attachments
  • Week 6: Glazing Techniques
What's Included:
  • 25 lbs of stoneware clay
  • Use of studio tools
  • Firing for up to 8 pieces

Frequently Asked Questions

Wear comfortable clothes that you don't mind getting dirty. Clay does wash out of most fabrics, but we recommend avoiding white. Please wear closed-toe shoes and tie back long hair. We provide aprons.

For all beginner classes, your first bag of clay (25 lbs) and use of studio tools are included in the price. Advanced students and open studio members must purchase clay from the studio (which covers the cost of firing).

The entire process takes time. After your piece is formed, it must dry for 1-2 weeks before bisque firing. After glazing, the final fire takes another week. Expect a minimum of 3-4 weeks from wet clay to finished piece.

Full refunds are provided for cancellations made at least 7 days before a class begins. Cancellations within 7 days receive studio credit. No-shows will not be refunded or credited.
