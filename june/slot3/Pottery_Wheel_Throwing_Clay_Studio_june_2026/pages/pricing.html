Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and memberships.

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with a prorated charge, while downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Monthly plans are billed month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime. Annual plans are billed yearly and offer a 20% discount compared to paying monthly.

Open studio gives you unstructured time to work on personal projects using our wheels, tools, and equipment. A studio technician is always available for basic assistance. Clay and firing are separate unless included in your plan.

Absolutely! We offer a single drop-in trial class for $65. It's a full 2-hour session where you'll learn to center and pull a bowl on the wheel. No membership required.

Yes! We offer a 15% student discount on all plans with a valid student ID. Groups of 4+ signing up together receive 10% off. Corporate team-building packages are also available — contact us for a custom quote.

After cancellation, you have 30 days to pick up any pieces still in progress or in storage. We'll fire any pieces currently in the kiln cycle at no extra charge. After 30 days, unclaimed items may be recycled.
