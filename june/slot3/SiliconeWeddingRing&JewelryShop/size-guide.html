Method Two

Measure with String

All you need is a piece of string or strip of paper and a ruler

Wrap the String

Mark the Overlap

Mark where the string or paper overlaps to complete a full circle

Measure Length

Lay flat and measure the length in millimeters — this is your circumference

Fit Tips

Everything you need to know about getting silicone ring sizing right

Silicone Stretches

Your BAND ring will conform to your finger over 1–2 days of wear. Don't panic if it feels slightly snug at first — it will break in beautifully.

Width Matters

Wider bands (8mm+) fit tighter than narrower ones. If ordering a wide profile, size up by half a size for the most comfortable fit.

Temperature Swells

Fingers swell in heat and shrink in cold. For the best fit, measure at the end of the day when your fingers are at their largest.

Free Exchanges

Got the wrong size? No problem — we exchange for free within 30 days, no questions asked. Your perfect fit is guaranteed.
