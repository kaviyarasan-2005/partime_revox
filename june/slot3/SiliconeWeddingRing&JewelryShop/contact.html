Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about sizing, exchanges, and safety

How does the lifetime warranty work?

We believe in our products. If your BAND breaks, tears, gets lost in the wild, or stretches out, we'll replace it. Just contact our support team with your order reference and we will ship a replacement ring directly to you.

What is your size exchange policy?

Getting the perfect fit is critical. We offer 100% free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Simply print out our size guide or test with a string, send a request through our contact page, and we'll send the new size immediately with a prepaid return envelope for the old one.

Is the silicone safe for all occupations?

Yes, BAND rings are constructed using medical-grade, 100% hypoallergenic, and non-conductive silicone. They are OSHA compliant and recommended for electricians, military personnel, medical workers, utility professionals, and athletes.
