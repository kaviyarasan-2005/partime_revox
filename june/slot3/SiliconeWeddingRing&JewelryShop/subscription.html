Subscription FAQ

Everything you need to know before committing

Can I change my ring style mid-subscription?

Yes! You can update your style and color preferences anytime through your account dashboard, up to 5 days before your scheduled delivery date.

What if I lose my ring before the replacement date?

Life happens! Subscribers can request an early replacement at a 40% discount through their account portal. We've got you covered even between scheduled deliveries.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime with zero fees or penalties. Log in to your account, navigate to Subscription Settings, and click "Cancel Plan." Your benefits continue until the end of the current billing period.

Can I skip a delivery?

Absolutely. You can skip any delivery up to 7 days before it's scheduled to ship. Your subscription continues on its regular cycle — skipping doesn't reset your billing date.

Do subscribers get discounts on one-time purchases?

12-Month Plan subscribers receive 10% off all one-time ring purchases, bundle upgrades, and accessory orders for the duration of their active subscription.

What happens to my old rings?

Every subscription includes our prepaid return envelope. Drop your used rings in, mail it back, and we'll recycle 100% of the silicone material into new products. You'll earn store credits for every return — the more you recycle, the more you save.
