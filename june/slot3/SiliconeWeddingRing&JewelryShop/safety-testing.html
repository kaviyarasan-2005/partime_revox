How We Test

Every batch of BAND rings goes through our 5-stage rigorous quality assurance protocol

1. Material Sourcing

100% medical-grade silicone sourced exclusively from certified, audited suppliers. Every shipment includes a Certificate of Conformance.

Batch Certificate Required

2. Tensile Strength

Stretch test: each ring must sustain 300% elongation without tearing or permanent deformation before it passes to the next stage.

300% Elongation Pass

3. Breakaway Force

Safety release calibration: every ring must break away cleanly under 15–20 lbs of concentrated force — enough to prevent ring avulsion injuries.

72-hour continuous exposure to industrial oils, cleaning solvents, acids, and chlorinated water. Rings must show zero degradation to pass.

Why Breakaway Matters

Ring avulsion — when a ring gets caught on machinery, equipment, or fixtures — causes thousands of serious finger injuries and amputations every year.

Traditional metal rings don't break under sudden force. They transfer that energy directly to your finger, causing severe lacerations, degloving injuries, or complete amputation.

BAND rings are engineered to release cleanly at a precisely calibrated force threshold — strong enough for everyday wear, but designed to protect you when it matters most.
