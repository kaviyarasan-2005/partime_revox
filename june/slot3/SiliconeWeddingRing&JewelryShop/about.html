Our Journey

Crafting the Future of Active Wear

We started with a simple question: why should active people risk their fingers for a symbol?

The BAND Story

Built for the Bold, Worn with Pride

Founded in Boulder, Colorado in 2024, BAND was born out of frustration. As outdoor enthusiasts, rock climbers, and gym-goers, we found ourselves constantly leaving our metal wedding rings at home or in lockers to avoid the very real danger of ring avulsion.

We realized that a wedding band shouldn't have to be tucked away. It should accompany you on every climb, every run, and every shift on the job. That's when we set out to create the ultimate active ring.

Made from hypoallergenic, medical-grade silicone, BAND rings combine rugged durability with modern styling. They look like metal bands but release safely under force, keeping you protected no matter where adventure takes you.

Our Core Values

What drives us every single day to engineer better products

Safety First

We do not compromise on security. Every product is calibrated to release under precise force thresholds to keep your hands safe.

Functional Comfort

A ring should feel like a second skin. We engineer for lightness, flexibility, and breathability, using materials that move with you.

Environmental Responsibility

We operate a closed-loop recycling program, turning returned silicone rings into new active wear, minimizing waste.
