The Process

How Glint Works

Our meticulous 4-step process ensures perfect adhesion, maximum protection, and a flawless finish every time.

Inspection

Paint thickness readings and surface inspection to assess condition and recommend the ideal package.

Decontamination

Multi-stage wash, clay bar treatment, and iron decontamination to create the perfect bonding surface.

Application

Professional coating application in controlled conditions ensuring complete, even coverage across all panels.

Curing & Delivery

IR lamp-assisted curing, quality inspection, and your vehicle returned with full warranty documentation.
