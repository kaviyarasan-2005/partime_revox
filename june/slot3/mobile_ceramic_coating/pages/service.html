Ceramic Coating

Our flagship nano-ceramic coating permanently bonds to your vehicle's clear coat, forming an ultra-hard, chemically-resistant layer that delivers unmatched gloss and protection. Unlike traditional wax or sealants, ceramic coating doesn't wash away — it lasts for years.

  • 9H Hardness Rating — Maximum scratch and swirl resistance
  • Extreme Hydrophobic Effect — Water beads and rolls off effortlessly
  • UV Protection — Prevents paint oxidation and color fading
  • Chemical Resistance — Protects against bird droppings, tree sap, acid rain

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Self-healing urethane film that provides physical protection against stone chips, road debris, and minor scratches. Available in gloss, matte, and satin finishes. When combined with ceramic coating, it creates an impenetrable shield.

  • Self-Healing Technology — Minor scratches disappear with heat
  • Impact Absorption — Protects against stone chips and road debris
  • Crystal Clarity — Virtually invisible with no yellowing
