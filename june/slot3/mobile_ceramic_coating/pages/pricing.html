FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Depending on the package, our coatings last from 1 year (Silver Shield) up to a lifetime (Diamond Armor). Longevity depends on maintenance frequency and driving conditions. Regular maintenance washes extend the coating's life significantly.
Ceramic coating amplifies the existing condition of your paint — it will enhance both the good and the bad. If your paint has visible swirls, scratches, or oxidation, we strongly recommend paint correction first to ensure a flawless result. Our Gold Gloss and Diamond Armor packages include correction.
Our service vans are fully self-contained with generators, lighting, water supply, and climate control. We can work in your driveway, parking garage, or any covered space with at least 12 feet of clearance. We require a shaded or covered area for optimal coating conditions.
We recommend waiting 24-48 hours before driving in light conditions and 7 days before washing or exposure to heavy rain. Our IR lamp-assisted curing significantly reduces the initial cure time. Full hardness is achieved at 14-21 days.
Our warranty covers coating delamination, significant loss of hydrophobic properties, and UV degradation. It does not cover paint damage from improper washing, scratches, or physical impacts. Warranty requires annual maintenance washes by our team to remain valid.
