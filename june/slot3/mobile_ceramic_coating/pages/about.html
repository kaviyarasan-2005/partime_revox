Our Story & Mission

Born from a passion for automotive excellence, Glint has redefined how car enthusiasts protect their most prized possessions.

Our Mission

Protecting What Matters Most

Glint was founded in 2016 by a team of automotive enthusiasts who believed that premium paint protection shouldn't require a shop appointment or a week without your car.

We pioneered mobile ceramic coating in Southern California, building a fleet of fully-equipped service vehicles that bring studio-quality results directly to our clients. Today, we operate across 8 states with over 50 certified technicians.

Our Journey

A Decade of Innovation

From a single service van to a nationwide fleet — our growth reflects the trust our customers place in us.

2016

The Beginning

Founded in Los Angeles with one service van and a vision to bring professional ceramic coating to enthusiasts.

2018

Rapid Expansion

Expanded to 5 cities, onboarded our first 10 certified technicians, and introduced the Diamond Armor package.

2020

Digital Dashboard Launch

Launched our customer dashboard allowing real-time curing tracking, paint thickness history, and warranty management.

National Presence
