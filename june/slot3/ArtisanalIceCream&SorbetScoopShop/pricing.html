Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and policies

Do you offer discounts for large orders?

Yes! Orders of 20+ pints receive 10% off, and 50+ pints receive 15% off. Catering packages also include volume-based pricing — contact us for a custom quote.

Are dairy-free and vegan flavours the same price?

Absolutely. All flavours — dairy-free, vegan, and traditional — are priced identically. We believe everyone deserves the same great value.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend ordering at least 48 hours ahead for standard cakes. For custom designs, please allow 5–7 days. During peak season (June–August), we recommend booking 2 weeks in advance.

Is there a minimum for catering orders?

Drop-off catering starts at 20 guests, Full Service at 30 guests, and Dessert Bar at 40 guests. For smaller events, our in-store party packages may be a better fit.

Do prices change seasonally?

Our core menu pricing stays consistent year-round. Seasonal and limited-edition flavours may have a small premium due to ingredient sourcing, but this is always clearly marked on the menu.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. For catering and event bookings, a 50% deposit is required to secure your date.
