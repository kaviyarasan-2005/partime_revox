The NOVA Journey

The NOVA Journey

It began with a copper pot, a farmhouse kitchen in Tuscany, and a revelation: that extraordinary ice cream requires nothing more than extraordinary ingredients, honest craft, and infinite patience.

Clara Montague returned from Italy in 2018 with a mission — to bring genuine artisanal gelato culture to our city, without compromise or shortcut. She opened NOVA's first boutique with six flavours, a handwritten menu, and a copper churn she'd shipped from Milan.

Today, NOVA serves hundreds of scoops daily, caters to weddings and corporate events across the region, and has been named one of the top ten independent ice cream shops in the country. None of that has changed how we make our ice cream. Not one thing.

What We Stand For

What We Believe

Four principles that guide every decision we make, from sourcing to serving

Quality First

Only the finest ingredients make it into our kitchen. We source locally where possible and globally when excellence demands it — never compromising on taste.

Sustainability

From compostable packaging to farm partnerships that prioritise animal welfare, we're committed to treading lightly while serving generously.

Community

NOVA exists because of this community. We support local farmers, employ locally, and give back through monthly charity scoops and school tasting programmes.

Innovation

Tradition grounds us, curiosity drives us forward. We constantly explore new flavour pairings, techniques, and plant-based innovations that push the boundaries of what ice cream can be.
