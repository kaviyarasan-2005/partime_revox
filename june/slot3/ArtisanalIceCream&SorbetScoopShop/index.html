Our Process

The Art of Ice Cream

Every scoop at NOVA begins long before it reaches your cone. We partner directly with local farms for the freshest dairy, hand-pick fruits at their peak, and churn in small batches to preserve every nuance of flavour.

Our recipes are developed by our artisan team — no shortcuts, no stabilisers, no compromise. Just pure, honest ice cream the way it was always meant to taste.

Source

Seasonal ingredients from trusted local farms and global artisan suppliers

Craft

Hand-churned in small batches using traditional copper-pot methods

Serve

Scooped fresh daily at the perfect temperature for maximum creaminess
