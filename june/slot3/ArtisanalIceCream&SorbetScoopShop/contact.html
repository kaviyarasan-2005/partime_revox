Quick Answers

Quick Answers

Before you reach out — see if we've already answered your question

Do you offer dairy-free options?
Yes, our full vegan collection uses coconut, oat, and almond milk bases — all equally creamy and indulgent.
How far in advance should I order a cake?
We recommend 48 hours for standard cakes, and 5–7 days for fully custom designs with specific decorations.
Do you cater outside the city?
Yes, we service within 50 miles with our refrigerated vehicles. Contact us for destinations beyond — we'll always try.
Can I taste flavours before ordering?
Absolutely. Visit our boutique for complimentary tastings — no appointment needed. We love watching people discover their new favourite.
