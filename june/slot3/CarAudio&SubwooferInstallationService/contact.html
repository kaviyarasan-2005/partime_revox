Quick FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking. Still have questions? Call us directly.

How soon can you come?

We typically have same-week availability, and in many cases next-day slots are open. Once you submit your booking request, we confirm your preferred time within 2 hours and send a technician profile with ETA.

Do I need to be present?

Yes — we ask that you or a trusted adult be present during the installation for security and so we can walk you through the finished system. The good news: you can relax at home or keep working while we handle everything.

Do you bring all the equipment?

Absolutely. Our vans are fully stocked with premium-brand audio equipment, wiring kits, mounting hardware, and diagnostic tools. You don't need to source or purchase anything — we handle it all, start to finish.
