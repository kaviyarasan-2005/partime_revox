Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile car audio installation service.

Our mobile technicians come directly to your home, workplace, or any preferred location. All we need is a flat surface and access to your vehicle. We bring all the tools, equipment, and supplies needed for the installation.
Installation time varies by package: Starter Package typically takes 1.5–2 hours, Advanced Package 2–3 hours, Premium Package 3–4 hours, and the Ultimate Package may take 5–6 hours. DSP tuning adds approximately 30–60 minutes.
We work with all major brands including Pioneer, JBL, Sony, Alpine, Kenwood, Kicker, and Rockford Fosgate. Our packages include curated equipment selections, but we can also install customer-supplied equipment.
All installations include a minimum 1-year warranty on labor. Premium and Ultimate packages include a 2-3 year warranty covering both parts and labor. We also handle all warranty claims directly with manufacturers on your behalf.
