Subwoofers

Subwoofer Installation & Calibration

Bass is the foundation of any great audio experience, but getting accurate, punchy low-end response in a moving vehicle requires precise engineering. We handle single/dual subwoofer installations, choosing the correct enclosure types (sealed for speed and accuracy, or ported for maximum output and depth).

Our team ensures your subwoofer is placed correctly to integrate seamlessly with your door speakers, matching phase alignment so that bass appears to hit from your dashboard rather than behind you.

Amplifiers

Amplifier Installation & Power Wiring

To sound clean and dynamic, upgraded speakers and subwoofers require stable power. We install multi-channel and monoblock class-D amplifiers, using pure oxygen-free copper (OFC) wiring to deliver stable current without voltage drops.

To prevent clipping and protect your hardware, we set amplifier gains using digital oscilloscopes, matching the amplifier's input sensitivity precisely to your head unit's RCA output voltage.

What's Included:

  • ✔OFC power and ground cables with inline ANL fuse holder at the battery
  • ✔Gain structure alignment to eliminate signal distortion and speaker heat
  • ✔Concealed routing of wiring behind kick panels and under carpeting

DSP Calibrations

Digital Signal Processor (DSP) Tuning

The cabin of a vehicle is a hostile environment for audio. Glass surfaces cause harsh reflections, plastic panels absorb frequencies, and you sit off-center, making the stereo imaging imbalanced. A Digital Signal Processor acts as the brain of your sound system, correcting these anomalies.

We use calibrated Real-Time Analyzers (RTA) to measure the acoustic output of your car. We then program the DSP with custom parametric EQ curves, time-alignment delays (so sound from all speakers hits your ears simultaneously), and active crossover cutoffs.

What's Included:

  • ✔RTA measurement of cabin acoustics at headrest height
  • ✔Delay calibration in milliseconds to center the soundstage on the driver
  • ✔Active crossover configuration to protect tweeters and mids from low-end overlap
View Details
Speaker Upgrades

Coaxial & Component Speaker Upgrades

Factory speakers are typically constructed with cheap paper cones and small magnets, resulting in muddy mids and virtually no treble clarity. We replace them with high-fidelity components made from carbon fiber, Kevlar, or poly-cones, paired with silk-dome tweeters.

We offer coaxial speakers for rear doors (where space is limited) and component speaker systems for front stages. We mount tweeters on your pillars or sails to lift your soundstage and create a wider sense of space.

What's Included:

  • ✔Custom composite mounting brackets to prevent air leaks around speakers
  • ✔Passive crossover network mounting and insulated spade terminations
  • ✔Acoustic foam fast-rings to seal speakers directly to the door panel

Acoustic Treatment

Door & Cabin Soundproofing

An upgraded speaker is only as good as the panel it is mounted in. Thin sheet metal doors act like bells, vibrating in response to speaker movements, which drains bass energy and adds distortion. Road and wind noise also leak in, masking your music's quiet passages.

We apply high-mass butyl rubber dampening sheets directly to the inner and outer sheet metal of your doors. This lowers the metal's resonant frequency, preventing panel rattle, and creating an enclosed 'cabinet' for your door speakers, which dramatically improves mid-bass output.

What's Included:

  • ✔Butyl dampening sheets applied with high-pressure roller tools
  • ✔Closed-cell foam (CCF) decoupling layers to eliminate panel friction
  • ✔Trunk floor and license plate dampening treatments to resolve bass rattles
