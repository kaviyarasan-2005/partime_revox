Booking

4 questions
Booking is simple and takes less than 5 minutes. You have three options:

1. Online Booking Form — Visit our Contact page and fill out the booking form with your vehicle make/model, preferred package, and available dates. We confirm within 2 hours.

2. Phone — Call us directly at 1-800-CAR-AUDIO (Mon–Sat, 8AM–8PM) and a team member will walk you through the process.

3. Email — Send your inquiry to hello@caraudiopro.com and we'll get back to you within 2 hours.
Pro tip: Book online for the fastest confirmation and to see real-time slot availability in your area.
We recommend booking at least 48–72 hours in advance to ensure technician availability in your area and to allow time for equipment sourcing if needed.

For popular weekend slots, we advise booking 5–7 days ahead. Competition-level packages with custom enclosures may require up to 2 weeks lead time for custom fabrication.
Heads up: Weekday morning appointments tend to have the best availability. Book early in the week for that week's service.
Yes — we offer flexible rescheduling at no charge, provided you give us at least 24 hours notice before your appointment time.

Rescheduling: Contact us by phone or email and we'll move your appointment to any available slot at no extra cost.

Cancellations: Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund of any deposit paid. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may forfeit the deposit, as the technician has been dispatched and equipment prepared.
Note: Custom-fabricated enclosures and special-order equipment may have different cancellation terms, which will be outlined at the time of order.

Same-day service is most readily available for our Starter and Bass Upgrade packages, as these require standard equipment we keep stocked in our service vehicles. Premium and Competition packages almost always require advance notice for equipment preparation.
Rush service: A same-day scheduling fee of $25 may apply depending on location and current technician routing.

Warranty

3 questions
Our warranty covers both parts and labor for defects in materials or workmanship resulting from our installation. Coverage periods by package:

• Starter — 1-year parts & labor
• Bass Upgrade — 2-year parts & labor
• Premium — 2-year full coverage
• Competition — 3-year coverage + 1 complimentary re-tune

What is NOT covered: Physical damage caused by accidents, floods, theft, modifications by third parties, or misuse (e.g., running at extreme volume levels that damage components beyond rated specifications).

Full warranty terms are documented in the Warranty page and provided in writing at the time of installation.
Making a warranty claim is straightforward:

Step 1. Contact us at 1-800-CAR-AUDIO or hello@caraudiopro.com and describe the issue.
Step 2. Have your installation date and job reference number ready (found on your warranty document).
Step 3. We'll schedule a technician to diagnose the issue — usually within 48–72 hours.
Step 4. Covered repairs or replacements are carried out at no cost to you.
Keep your warranty card: Your warranty documentation includes a QR code that fast-tracks your claim. If you've lost it, we can look up your record by phone number or vehicle plate.
Yes — our warranty is attached to the vehicle, not the owner. If you sell your car within the warranty period, the remaining coverage transfers automatically to the new owner at no additional charge.

To formally transfer the warranty, the new owner simply needs to contact us with the vehicle VIN and the original installation date. We update our records and issue a new warranty card in their name. This is a great selling point when listing your vehicle!

DSP stands for Digital Signal Processor — a dedicated computer chip that controls every aspect of your audio signal before it reaches your speakers and subwoofer.

What it does:
• Time alignment — adjusts the timing of each speaker so sound reaches your ears simultaneously, creating a focused "stage" in front of you
• Equalization (EQ) — compensates for the acoustic imperfections of your car's interior (glass, fabric, plastic reflections)
• Crossover management — ensures bass frequencies go only to the subwoofer and highs only to tweeters, preventing distortion
• Dynamic range control — keeps audio clear at both quiet and loud volumes

Why you need it: Even the most expensive speakers will sound mediocre without proper tuning. A professionally calibrated DSP can make a $500 system sound better than a $2,000 untuned system.
Included in: DSP tuning is included in our Bass Upgrade, Premium, and Competition packages. It can also be added to the Starter package for $99.
