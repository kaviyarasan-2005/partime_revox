Coverage Details

What's Covered

Transparency is important to us. Here's a clear breakdown of what our warranty covers and what it doesn't.

What's Covered
  • Parts & Hardware
    All installed audio components — subwoofers, amplifiers, speakers, wiring, and mounting hardware defective within the warranty period.
  • Labor Costs
    All technician labor for warranty-related repairs and reinstallations at no charge to you.
  • DSP Re-Tuning
    Professional DSP recalibration visits if sound performance degrades due to installation-related causes.
  • Factory Defects
    Manufacturing faults in audio equipment supplied and installed by CarAudio Pro technicians.
  • Electrical Faults
    Wiring, grounding, and electrical connectivity issues arising from our installation work.
What's Not Covered
  • Physical Damage
    Damage caused by accidents, impacts, water ingress not related to our installation, or mishandling of the equipment.
  • Improper Use
    Damage resulting from operating equipment beyond rated specifications, clipping the amplifier, or sustained overdriving.
  • Third-Party Modifications
    Any modifications, repairs, or tampering performed by a party other than CarAudio Pro that voids the original installation integrity.
  • Theft or Loss
    Stolen or lost equipment is not covered under the warranty. We recommend comprehensive vehicle insurance for this.

Not sure? If you're uncertain whether your issue is covered, contact our support team — we'll always try to help. We review every case individually.

How To Claim

Claims Process

Filing a warranty claim is simple and hassle-free. We aim to resolve all claims within 48 hours of contact.

01
Contact Us

Reach our warranty support team by phone, email, or live chat. Have your installation receipt and service ID ready for faster processing.

02
Describe the Issue

Our team will ask you to describe the fault in detail. Share photos or a short video if possible — this speeds up diagnosis significantly.

03
Technician Dispatched

A certified technician is scheduled and dispatched to your location at a time convenient for you — no shop visits required.

04
Resolution & Sign-Off

The technician repairs or replaces the faulty component. You'll receive a digital sign-off confirming the resolution and updated warranty record.
