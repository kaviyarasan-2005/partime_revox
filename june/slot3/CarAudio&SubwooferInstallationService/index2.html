Acoustic Calibration

Why Acoustic Calibration Matters

Professional hardware is only half the battle. Digital signal processing unlocks true audio potential.

Time Alignment

Speakers are at different distances from your ears. We delay sound by milliseconds so all frequencies hit you simultaneously.

RTA Equalization

We use real-time analyzers and calibrated microphones to identify frequency spikes and dips inside your cabin, smoothing out response.

Crossover Filtering

Send sub-bass frequencies to the trunk and treble to tweeters. Prevent distortion and extend speaker life span.
