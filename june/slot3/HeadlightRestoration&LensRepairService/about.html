Our Story

The LUME Story

LUME was born in 2018 from a simple frustration. Our founder, Daniel Carter, watched his mother pay $340 to replace perfectly restorable headlights — simply because no convenient alternative existed. He spent the next year developing a mobile restoration system that would bring professional results directly to customers' doorsteps.

Starting with one van and a route through Austin, Texas, LUME grew through word of mouth into a multi-city operation serving thousands of satisfied customers. Today we operate across 50+ service areas with a team of 30+ certified technicians — all united by one mission.

Our Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Two guiding principles that define every decision we make and every service we deliver.

Our Mission

"To make professional headlight restoration accessible to every vehicle owner — with unmatched convenience, transparency, and guaranteed results."

We exist to save vehicle owners money, improve road safety, and deliver a premium service experience without requiring them to leave their home or workplace.

Our Vision

"A world where every vehicle drives with optimal visibility and safety — because clear headlights shouldn't be a luxury."

We envision LUME as the national standard for mobile headlight care — trusted by individual owners and major fleet operators alike, expanding to every corner of the country.

Quality First

Every restoration is held to our strict 10-point quality standard before we consider the job complete.

Safety Driven

Clear headlights reduce accident risk by up to 40% at night. We take that responsibility seriously.

Eco-Conscious

Restoration vs. replacement saves thousands of headlight units from landfill every year.

Community Focused

We hire locally, train extensively, and give back through free services for community organizations.
