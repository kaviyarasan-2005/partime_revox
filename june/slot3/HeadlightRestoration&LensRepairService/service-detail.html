Our Methodology

The 3-Step Process

How we achieve factory-level results that last for years.

1

Oxidation Removal

We begin by masking the surrounding paint. Then, using 4 specialized grits of wet sandpaper, we completely strip away the damaged, yellowed factory coating and oxidized plastic.

2

Optical Polishing

Using a rotary polisher and specialized cutting compounds, we refine the scratches left from sanding. This brings the bare polycarbonate lens to a 100% optically clear state.

3

UV Hard Coat Cure

Clarity means nothing without protection. We apply a proprietary resin clear coat and cure it using intensive UV lamps, cross-linking the polymers into a durable, permanent shell.
