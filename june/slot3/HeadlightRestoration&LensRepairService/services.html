Our Process

Our Restoration Process

A meticulous, 5-stage system developed over thousands of successful restorations.

1

Inspection & Photo Assessment

We evaluate oxidation depth, crack severity, and UV damage to determine the optimal treatment plan.

~5 minutes
2

Surface Preparation & Cleaning

Thorough decontamination and masking to protect surrounding paint and trim during the process.

~10 minutes
3

Sanding & Polishing (3 Stages)

Progressive wet sanding (400→800→1500 grit) removes damage layers. Machine polishing restores gloss and clarity.

~45–60 minutes
4

UV Protective Coating Application

Professional-grade sealant is applied in controlled layers, bonding to the polycarbonate at the molecular level.

~20 minutes
5

Final Inspection & Warranty Certificate

A thorough quality check is performed and a 2-year UV protection warranty certificate is issued digitally.

~5 minutes
