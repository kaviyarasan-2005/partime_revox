Common Questions

We bring a fully-equipped service van to your location — home, office, or anywhere convenient. Our technician performs the brake service on-site using professional-grade equipment, just like a traditional shop but without the hassle of dropping off your car.
All our services come with a 12-month / 12,000-mile warranty covering both parts and labor. If you experience any issues within the warranty period, we'll come back and fix it at no additional cost.
Most brake services take between 1-2 hours depending on the scope. Rotor resurfacing for one axle typically takes about 45-60 minutes. A full vehicle service including all four rotors takes approximately 2 hours.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. Payment is collected after the service is completed to your satisfaction.
We service most passenger vehicles including sedans, SUVs, trucks, and minivans from all major manufacturers. This includes domestic (Ford, Chevy, Dodge), Japanese (Toyota, Honda, Nissan), and European (BMW, Mercedes, VW) brands. We service vehicles from 1998-present.
