Our Resurfacing Process

Five precision steps to perfectly smooth rotors

Inspection

Visual and physical inspection of brake components for wear, damage, and contamination

Measurement

Precision micrometer measurements of rotor thickness, runout, and parallelism

Machining

Computer-controlled brake lathe cuts resurface both sides to factory-smooth finish

Quality Check

Post-machining verification ensures specifications meet OEM tolerances

Installation

Rotors reinstalled with proper torque specs and brake system reassembled
