Our Story

BRAKE was founded in 2019 by a team of ASE-certified technicians who saw a simple problem: getting your brakes serviced meant losing an entire day at the shop. We knew there had to be a better way.

Starting with a single service van and a passion for precision, we built BRAKE on the idea that professional brake service should come to you — not the other way around. Our founder, Mike Reynolds, spent 15 years working in dealership service departments before deciding to bring that same quality directly to customers' driveways.

Today, BRAKE operates a fleet of fully-equipped mobile workshops, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the metro area. Our commitment to quality, convenience, and transparency has made us the region's most trusted mobile brake service provider.
