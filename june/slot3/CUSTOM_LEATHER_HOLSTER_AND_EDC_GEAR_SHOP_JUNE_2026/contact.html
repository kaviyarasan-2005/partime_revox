Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our custom leather work

Standard orders take 3-4 weeks, Premium orders 2-3 weeks, and Elite orders 1-2 weeks. Lead times may vary during peak seasons. We'll provide an estimated completion date when you place your order.

We need your firearm or gear model, preferred holster type (IWB, OWB, appendix, shoulder), leather choice, color preference, and any special requirements (left/right hand, cant angle, tooling patterns). Photos of your gear are always helpful.

Yes, we ship to most countries. International shipping is handled via USPS Priority Mail International or UPS Worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times are calculated at checkout. Please note that import duties and taxes are the buyer's responsibility.

Because each piece is custom-made to your specifications, we do not accept returns on custom orders. However, if there's a fit issue or defect, we'll remake or repair the piece at no charge under our craftsman warranty. Standard stock items can be returned within 30 days.

Absolutely. We welcome workshop visits by appointment. You can see your leather options in person, discuss your project face-to-face, and watch the crafting process. Contact us to schedule a visit during business hours.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Venmo, and bank wire transfers. Custom orders require a 50% deposit to begin production, with the balance due before shipping.
