Our Services

From concept to carry — complete leather crafting solutions for every need

Custom Holster Services

Every holster is wet-molded to your exact firearm model for a precision fit

IWB (Inside Waistband)

Concealed carry holsters designed for comfort and invisibility. Available in full-coverage and minimalist tuckable designs. Adjustable cant from 0-15 degrees.

Full GrainTuckableAdjustable Cant

OWB (Outside Waistband)

Open carry and range holsters with reinforced belt loops. Available with hand-tooled borders, basket weave, or smooth finish. Thumb break optional.

Hand-TooledThumb BreakBelt Loop

Appendix Carry

Ultra-slim appendix holsters with integrated wedge for body contour. Designed for quick draw from the front of the waistband. Sweat guard included.

Slim ProfileWedge SystemSweat Guard

Shoulder Holsters

Classic shoulder rig systems with padded harness and tie-down straps. Magazine carrier on opposite side for balance. Available in horizontal or vertical draw.

Padded HarnessMag CarrierTie-Down

EDC Gear Accessories

Leather carriers built to fit your daily tools with precision and style

Magazine Pouches

Single and double mag carriers with adjustable tension. Fits all popular calibers and magazine sizes.

Knife Sheaths

Custom-molded sheaths for fixed blades and folding knives. Belt loop, scout carry, and dangler options.

Flashlight Holders

Snug-fit leather carriers for tactical and everyday flashlights. Open-top and flap-closure styles.

Multi-Tool Cases

Leather cases for Leatherman, Victorinox, and similar. Side-entry and top-flap designs with snap closures.
