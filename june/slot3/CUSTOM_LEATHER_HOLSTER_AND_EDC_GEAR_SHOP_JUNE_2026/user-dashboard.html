Measurement Guidelines

To ensure a perfect custom fit, please follow these guidelines when measuring and photographing your gear:

  • Use Precise Tools: Where possible, use a digital caliper or a clear ruler for measurements.
  • Clear Photos: Take photos on a flat, neutral background with a ruler or coin in the frame for scale.
  • Multiple Angles: Upload at least one side profile and one top-down view of the gear.
  • Specify Attachments: Mention any aftermarket sights, lights, or optics in the notes section.
