Critical Safety Warning

The Danger of Misalignment

Even a 1-degree shift in a front camera or radar angle can result in the vehicle scanning yards off-center at highway speeds. ADAS sensors rely on precise geometric reference points that change after any minor collision, wheel alignment, or windshield replacement.

  • False Braking Triggers: Off-center radar mounting causes the system to brake for vehicles in adjacent lanes.
  • Lane Keep Drifting: Incorrect windshield camera calibrations lead to dangerous over-steering into oncoming traffic.
