Behind The Craft

How We Create Wearable Art

A rigorous, multi-week preservation process ensures your natural keepsakes last a lifetime.

1

Petal Selection & Drying

Fresh blossoms are selected at peak vibrancy and dried for 10-14 days in professional-grade silica sand or botanical wood presses.

2

Bezel Mounting & Layout

Artisans construct custom 925 sterling silver frames or select molds, carefully placing each stem using microscopic tweezers.

3

Layered UV Curing

Resin is poured in micro-layers to prevent flower scorching. UV chambers eliminate micro-bubbles for crystal clarity.

4

Polishing & Packaging

Each cured piece is wet-sanded, diamond-buffed, mounted on custom chains, and wrapped in organic cotton jewelry boxes.
