Mailing Instructions

How to Ship Fresh Flowers

Ensure your delicate blooms arrive safely at our studio by following these three essential packing steps.

1

Hydrate & Trim

Cut stems at an angle and wrap the bottom 2 inches in a damp (not soaking wet) paper towel. Secure with a rubber band.

2

Pack with Care

Place the bouquet in a sturdy cardboard box surrounded by crumpled packing paper or tissue. Do NOT use plastic wrap or bubble wrap, as it traps moisture and causes mold.

3

Overnight Express

Ship the box via FedEx or UPS Overnight. Avoid shipping on Fridays, as packages may sit in warm warehouses over the weekend.
