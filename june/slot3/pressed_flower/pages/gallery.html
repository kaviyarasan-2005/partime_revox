Methodology

How We Create The Magic

A multi-stage, delicate process requiring botanical expertise, meticulous metal fabrication, and precise curing.

1

Silica Dehydration

Raw flowers are carefully placed in premium silica gel chambers for 7-14 days to pull moisture out without losing structural integrity.

2

Bezel Fabrication

Raw 925 sterling silver wire is bent and soldered into custom bezels, polished to a mirrorshine to hold the resin mold perfectly.

3

Layered Pouring

Flowers are arranged inside the bezel, and high-stabilizer bio-resin is poured in multiple thin layers to prevent microbubble formation.

4

UV Curing & Polish

The cured block is heated to release internal tension, and then hand-sanded and buffed using botanical wax to a glass-like shine.

Quality Materials

Designed to Last

We believe that botanical heirlooms should survive generations. We hold our materials to the highest professional standard to ensure your keepsake never degrades:

  • UV-Resistant Resin: Infused with professional UV absorbers (HALS) that block solar yellowing.
  • 925 Recycled Silver: Environmentally friendly metal bezel frames bent and soldered entirely by hand.
  • Anti-Bubble Technology: Heated pressure chambers cure the resin, leaving zero air bubbles around the petals.
