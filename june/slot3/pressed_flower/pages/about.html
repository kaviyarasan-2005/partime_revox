Our Artisan Story

Rooted in nature, crafted by hand. Petali preserves memory fragments inside bespoke wearable keepsakes.

Since 2018

Capturing Fleeting Beauty

Founded in a small greenhouse workshop, Petali grew from a passion for floriculture and preservation crafts. We noticed how wedding bouquets, graduation corsages, and memorial petals are often discarded, despite their immense emotional values.

Our studio focuses on transforming these temporary expressions into archival-quality resin ornaments. Over the past eight years, we have refined our silica drying chambers and micro-curing processes to ensure color retention and bubble-free castings.

Our Principles

Guided by Sustainability

We treat every plant and resin pour with ultimate respect, adhering to eco-conscious practices.

Ethical Sourcing

We only pick plants from our certified Sage Green garden or partner with local organic florists who practice carbon-offset farming.

Eco-Polymers

Our casting liquid consists of up to 45% bio-based carbon contents, reducing petroleum consumption during production cycles.

Recycled Metals

All bezel frames, ring blanks, and matching chains are constructed from verified 925 recycled sterling silver and gold fill.

Zero Waste

Even failed casts are repurposed! We crush them to create terrazzo resin coasters and studio decorative wall mountings.
