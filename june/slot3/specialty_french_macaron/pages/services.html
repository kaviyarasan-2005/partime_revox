How We Work

Our Artisan Process

Every macaron follows our strict 48-hour production process, from aging the shells to final quality inspection.

1

Ingredient Sourcing

Premium almond flour, aged eggs, and seasonal flavor ingredients sourced weekly from trusted suppliers.

2

Shell Creation

Macaronage by hand, precise piping, 30-minute rest, and baking at exact temperatures for perfect feet and smooth tops.

3

Ganache & Filling

Freshly prepared ganache, buttercream, or compote fillings — each recipe developed and tested by Chef Isabelle.

4

24hr Rest & Dispatch

Assembled macarons rest for 24 hours to achieve the perfect chewy texture before quality inspection and careful packing.
