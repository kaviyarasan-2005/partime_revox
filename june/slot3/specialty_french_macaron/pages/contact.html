Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most commonly asked questions about orders, shipping, and dietary options.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes! We offer overnight nationwide shipping to ensure your macarons arrive fresh. Orders placed before 12 PM EST ship the same day.

How long do macarons stay fresh?

For the best experience, keep them refrigerated and consume within 5 days. Let them sit at room temperature for 15 minutes before enjoying.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Our macarons are naturally made with almond flour and are gluten-free. However, they are prepared in a kitchen that also handles gluten.

Can I customize colors for my event?

Absolutely! We offer custom pantone color matching for weddings and corporate events. Please mention this in the contact form above.
