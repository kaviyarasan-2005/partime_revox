Fully Bespoke

Custom Leather Journals

Begin with a blank canvas. Choose your leather type (full-grain, top-grain, or suede), colour, finish, and size. Select your paper weight from 80gsm writing paper to 120gsm fountain-pen-safe ivory sheets.

Add personalisation — hand-stamped initials, embossed artwork, or a hand-painted edge in a custom colour. Each journal takes 5–10 business days to complete and arrives gift-wrapped.

For the Artist

Artist Sketchbooks

Our sketchbooks use Coptic lay-flat binding so you can work across a full double-page spread without the paper springing back. Available in A5, A4, and A3 sizes.

Paper ranges from 200gsm watercolour paper to 130gsm drawing cartridge. The refillable cover means your journal grows with your practice — simply order a new paper block and thread it in yourself, or send it back to us.

Book Restoration

Whether it's a family heirloom, a crumbling antique, or a beloved journal that has seen better days — our restoration service treats every piece as the irreplaceable object it is.

Services include spine re-sewing, cover rebinding, leather conditioning and repair, page deacidification, and gold tooling restoration. Free initial assessment — simply send us photographs to begin.

How It Works

From Brief to Bindery

01

Share Your Brief

Fill in our contact form or email us with your requirements, quantities, and any inspiration images. We'll respond within one business day.

02

Receive a Quote

We'll send a detailed quote covering materials, timeline, and any customisation options. No obligation, no pressure.

03

We Make It

Once approved, our artisans begin work. You'll receive progress photos for large orders. Each piece is quality-checked before wrapping.

04

Delivered to You

Your journal ships tracked and insured worldwide in our signature gift box. Domestic delivery: 1–2 days. International: 3–10 days.
