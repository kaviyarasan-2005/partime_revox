Our Beginning

From a Garage Workshop to a Global Studio

In 2008, master bookbinder Elena Whitmore set up a small workshop in her Clerkenwell garage with two bench knives, a bone folder, and a vision: to revive the art of hand-bound leather journals in an age of mass production.

What began as a craft practiced in solitude became a community. Word spread through London's art schools, writing clubs, and design studios. Within three years, the garage became a proper studio. Within five, journals were shipping to 40 countries.

Today, our team of eight artisans — each trained in traditional European bookbinding — continues that founding practice. Every single journal still leaves our hands, not a factory floor.

2008

The First Stitch

Elena Whitmore binds her first leather journal in a Clerkenwell garage. First 12 journals sell within a week at a local market.

2011

The Studio Opens

Moved to 14 Bindery Lane. First full-time apprentice hired. Corporate orders begin as Penguin Press commissions 200 journals.

2015

The Restoration Wing

Book restoration service launched. Team grows to five artisans. First international award from the Guild of Book Workers.

2020

Sustainable Leather Pledge

100% of leather sourced from certified sustainable tanneries. FSC-certified paper introduced across all lines.

2026

Today

8 artisans, 84 countries, over 12,000 journals made. Still hand-stitched. Still hand-signed. Still made to last a lifetime.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

Three principles guide every decision we make — from material sourcing to the knot we use to finish a binding.

Craft Over Commerce

We will never sacrifice quality for speed or scale. Every journal takes the time it takes. We cap our output before we cut corners.

Sustainable by Design

Every hide comes from a certified sustainable tannery. Our paper is FSC-certified. Our packaging is recycled and plastic-free. Good craft shouldn't cost the earth.

Community First

We train apprentices through our in-house program, donate journals to schools, and partner with writing charities across the UK. Every purchase funds this work.
