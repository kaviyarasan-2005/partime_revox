Our Heritage

Where Leather Meets Artistry

Born from a deep love of the written word and the feel of quality leather, our bindery has been creating heirloom-quality journals and sketchbooks since 2008. Every piece is shaped by skilled hands, not machines.

We source only full-grain and vegetable-tanned leather from sustainable tanneries, pairing it with acid-free, archival paper that will outlast generations.

How It's Made

The Craft Behind Every Journal

From raw hide to finished heirloom — a meticulous process refined over 18 years.

01

Material Selection

We hand-select every hide from sustainable tanneries, choosing only full-grain leather with natural character marks.

02

Hand-Cutting & Tooling

Each piece is traced, cut by hand, and tooled with traditional stamp patterns before conditioning and dyeing.

03

Bookbinding

Signatures are Coptic or Smyth-sewn by hand using linen thread, creating a lay-flat binding that grows more beautiful with use.

04

Quality & Dispatch

Every journal passes a 12-point quality check before being wrapped in tissue and dispatched in our signature gift box.
