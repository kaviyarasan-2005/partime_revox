About AquaShine Marine

Miami's most trusted mobile marine detailing experts since 2009

Our Story

Born From a Passion for the Sea

AquaShine Marine was founded in 2009 by Carlos Mendez — a Miami-born boater who grew tired of subpar detailing services that didn't understand the unique demands of marine environments. Armed with IDA certification and a truck full of professional-grade equipment, he set out to build the region's most trusted mobile marine care company.

Today, our crew of eight certified specialists serves marinas, private slips, yacht clubs, and waterfront homes across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We've detailed everything from 16-foot bay boats to 90-foot superyachts.

Our Journey

15 Years of Marine Excellence

2009

AquaShine Founded

Carlos Mendez launches AquaShine from a single truck, servicing Miami Beach marinas with hand-wax and basic detailing packages.

2013

IDA Certification & Fleet Expansion

Obtained IDA certification, added 3 specialist detailers, and expanded to Broward and Palm Beach counties.

2017

Ceramic Coating Launch

Introduced professional ceramic nano-coating services for yachts — the first mobile marine detailer in South Florida to offer this service.
