Pricing FAQs

Pricing Questions

Are the prices per vessel or per hour?
All our pricing is flat-rate per vessel, not per hour. This means you know exactly what you're paying upfront, with no surprise charges for jobs that take longer than expected.
How does pricing work for larger vessels?
Our listed prices apply to vessels up to 30ft. For 31–50ft vessels, add 35%. For vessels over 50ft, we provide a custom quote based on an in-person or photo assessment of the vessel.
Do you offer monthly maintenance contracts?
Yes! We offer monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly maintenance contracts with a 15–20% discount off standard rates. Contracts include priority scheduling and a dedicated technician who gets to know your vessel.
Is there a travel fee for distant locations?
There is no travel fee for locations within our standard service area (Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach Counties). For locations beyond 25 miles from our base in Miami, a nominal fuel surcharge may apply.
