How We Work

Our Service Process

1. Vessel Assessment
We begin every job with a thorough visual inspection of your vessel, noting existing scratches, oxidation levels, teak condition, and interior state. This ensures we apply the right products and techniques for your specific needs.
2. Service Planning
Based on the assessment, we recommend the optimal service package and products. We discuss your expectations, timeline, and any specific areas of concern before we begin any work.
3. Professional Execution
Our certified technicians execute the agreed service using only professional-grade, marine-safe products. We work methodically from top to bottom, using machine polishers, rotary buffers, and specialized marine tools.
4. Quality Inspection
Before we pack up, we conduct a rigorous quality control walk-around with you present. We document before and after photos for your records and provide care tips to maintain your vessel's condition.
5. Maintenance Schedule
We set you up with a customized maintenance calendar, recommend ideal detailing frequencies based on your usage, and offer priority scheduling for repeat clients with discounted maintenance packages.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical boat detailing take?
A standard full detail on a 25–30 foot vessel typically takes 3–5 hours. Larger yachts, ceramic coating applications, and gel coat restoration projects may require a full day or multiple visits depending on the vessel's condition.
Do you bring all your own equipment and water?
Yes! We are fully self-contained. Our vehicles carry generators, freshwater tanks, professional polishers, and all necessary products. You only need to provide dock access — we handle everything else.
Which areas do you service?
We service Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach Counties — including all major marinas, yacht clubs, private docks, and waterfront residences. Contact us for service outside these areas; we may be able to accommodate you.
How long does ceramic coating last on a boat?
Our marine-grade ceramic coatings are rated for 3–5 years of protection when properly maintained. We include a maintenance kit and schedule recommendations with every ceramic coating service to maximize longevity.
Do I need to be present during the service?
You don't need to be present for the entire service. We just need access to the vessel and dock at the start. We'll send you progress updates and contact you when the service is complete for a final walkthrough inspection.
