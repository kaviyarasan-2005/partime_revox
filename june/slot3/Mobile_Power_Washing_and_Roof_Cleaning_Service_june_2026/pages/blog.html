Pro Tips from Our Experts

Start from the Bottom

Always wash from bottom to top to prevent streaking. Rinse from top to bottom for a clean finish.

Best Time to Wash

Early morning or late afternoon is ideal. Avoid midday sun as cleaning solutions can dry too quickly.

Protect Your Plants

Wet your landscaping before and after washing. This dilutes any cleaning solution that contacts plants.

Annual Maintenance

Schedule professional cleaning at least once per year to prevent buildup and maintain your property value.
