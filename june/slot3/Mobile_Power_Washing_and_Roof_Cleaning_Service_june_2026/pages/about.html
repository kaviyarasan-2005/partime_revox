Our Story

From a single pressure washer to a fleet of professional vehicles — discover the passion behind SHINE.

Built on Hard Work & Clean Results

SHINE was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: make professional exterior cleaning accessible to every homeowner. What started as a one-man operation with a pickup truck and a pressure washer has grown into a full-service cleaning company serving over 2,000 properties annually.

Our founder, Marcus Williams, noticed that most power washing companies were either too expensive or delivered inconsistent results. He set out to build a company that combines competitive pricing with uncompromising quality — and SHINE was born.

Today, our team of certified technicians uses the latest equipment and eco-friendly solutions to transform properties across the greater Springfield area. Every job is backed by our satisfaction guarantee.
