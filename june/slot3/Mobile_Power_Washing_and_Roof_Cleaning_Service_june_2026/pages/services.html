Service Details

Everything you need to know about each of our services.

Our house washing service includes full exterior soft-wash treatment, window frame cleaning, gutter face cleaning, and post-wash inspection. Duration: 2-4 hours depending on home size. Warranty: 6-month clean guarantee. All biodegradable cleaning solutions included.

We use a low-pressure soft-wash system (under 100 PSI) with a specialized cleaning solution that kills algae, moss, and lichen at the root. No high pressure ever touches your shingles. Duration: 1-3 hours. 12-month warranty against black streak return.

We use surface cleaners rated at 3,500 PSI for even, streak-free results on concrete and pavers. Pre-treatment for oil stains included. Post-clean sealer application available as an add-on. Duration: 1-2 hours for standard driveways.

4-step process: 1) Sodium percarbonate stripping, 2) Low-pressure cleaning, 3) Oxalic acid brightening, 4) Dry and optional stain application. Safe for all wood types including cedar, pine, and composite. Duration: 4-6 hours including drying time.
