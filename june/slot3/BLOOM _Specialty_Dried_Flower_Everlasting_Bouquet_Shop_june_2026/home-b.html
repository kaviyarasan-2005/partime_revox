Care Guide

Keep your dried flowers away from direct sunlight to prevent colors from fading over time. Indirect, natural light is perfectly fine.

Avoid placing arrangements in highly humid areas like bathrooms. Moisture can cause the stems to mold or droop.

To remove dust, use a hairdryer on the lowest, cool setting held at a distance, or gently brush with a soft makeup brush.

With proper care, your everlasting bouquet will maintain its beauty for 1 to 3 years or even longer.
