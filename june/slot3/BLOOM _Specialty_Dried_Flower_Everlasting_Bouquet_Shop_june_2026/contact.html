Our Bespoke Process

From the initial consultation to the final placement, we work closely with you to curate everlasting botanicals that complement your space perfectly.

01. Consultation

Share your vision, color palettes, and space requirements with us. We'll discuss custom sizing, vase options, and preferred botanicals to match your aesthetic.

02. Design & Proposal

Our artisans curate a custom selection of high-quality dried florals, presenting a detailed design proposal and color board for your approval.

03. Handcraft & Delivery

Upon approval, we meticulously assemble your arrangement by hand. We safely pack and deliver your order, offering installation support for larger works.
