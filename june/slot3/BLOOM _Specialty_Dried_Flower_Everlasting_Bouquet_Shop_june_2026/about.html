Our Story

Rooted in passion, grown with purpose.

"We saw beauty where others saw an end. A dried flower is a memory kept alive, a moment suspended in time."

2018

Founded in a small kitchen

BLOOM started as a passion project, experimenting with hanging flowers from ceiling beams and perfecting the natural air-drying process to retain vibrant colors.

2020

The First Workshop

Outgrowing the kitchen, we moved into our first official studio space—a rustic barn where we could scale our preservation techniques and start offering custom wedding florals.

2022

Online Launch

We expanded our reach, launching our online boutique to ship our everlasting bouquets nationwide, bringing sustainable beauty to homes everywhere.

Today

A Thriving Atelier

Now a team of dedicated floral artisans, we continue to innovate in sustainable floristry, offering bespoke arrangements, large-scale event styling, and workshops.
